What is TDEE?

TDEE = Total Daily Energy Expenditure.
It is an estimation of the total energy a person uses in a day.


How Do I Lose Weight?

To lose weight, 500 kcal / 2000 kJ deficit is usually a good place to start.
Simply calculate your TDEE above and then subtract 500 kcal from it - the result is going to be your daily calorie income to lose weight.


How Do I Gain Weight?

To gain weight, 500 kcal / 2000 kJ surplus is usually a good place to start.
Simply calculate your TDEE above and then add 500 kcal to it - the result is going to be your daily calorie income to gain weight.
